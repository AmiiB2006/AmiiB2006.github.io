How to make Chicken Wings with Potato Wedges

Ingredients
  • Frozen chicken wings
  • Sesoning
  • Oil
  • Potatoes

Steps
  1. put chicken wings into hot water
  2. slipt potatoes in half and cut them into four after cleaning them
  3. season wings with black pepper, garlic powder, maggi, paprika, parsley, and curry powder. Season potatoes with black pepper, garlic powder, maggi, parsley, red pepper flakes, and parmasean
  4. fry wings and bake potatoes
  5. plate them
